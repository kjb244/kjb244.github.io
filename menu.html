Measure, Grind, Brew, Repeat.

Our focus at Cuplux is brewing beautiful, delicious coffees for folks on the go. We proudly serve Stumptown Coffee and feature guest roasters exclusively from the Carolinas.

Our craft beverage menu includes a select variety of coffees, teas and espresso-based beverages, all made from the best ingredients possible.

Food wise, we offer a small selection of local pastries that pair well with our drinks, are portable, and most importantly - are delicious. Enjoy!
